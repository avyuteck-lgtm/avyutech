Company Overview

Who We Are

Avyu Technologies is a cybersecurity and compliance solutions provider focused on developing practical, effective tools for cyber threat detection, digital investigations, and regulatory compliance.

We serve enterprises, law enforcement agencies, and small businesses — delivering purpose-built security products that address real-world challenges in the Indian cybersecurity landscape and beyond.

Mission

To build innovative cybersecurity tools that help organizations protect data and strengthen digital trust.

Vision

To become a trusted provider of cybersecurity and investigation technology solutions across India and globally.

Core Focus Areas

What We Do

Four pillars that define how Avyu Technologies delivers security value to customers and partners.

Cybersecurity Solutions

Proactive tools to detect, investigate, and respond to cyber threats — from phishing to advanced persistent threats.

Data Privacy Compliance

End-to-end support for India's DPDP Act compliance, including data mapping, consent management, and DPIA assessments.

Digital Investigation Tools

Specialized platforms for CDR analysis, crime intelligence, and suspect network mapping to support law enforcement.

Lightweight audit tools that help SMBs evaluate their security posture and prioritize remediation efforts.
